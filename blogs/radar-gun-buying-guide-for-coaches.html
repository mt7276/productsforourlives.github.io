Radar Gun Buying Guide for Coaches & Scouts

Accuracy, side-angle tolerance, and data logging explained

Save time and budget by matching the radar gun to how you coach. We cover accuracy specs, beam angle, app integration, and durability so you can choose the right tool for bullpens, showcases, or games.

Specs that matter

  • Accuracy: Look for ±1 mph and published testing.
  • Side-angle tolerance: Narrow beams handle foul-line positioning; wide beams need behind-the-plate spots.
  • Acquisition speed: Fast lock reduces missed pitches during games.
  • Data workflow: App/Bluetooth export saves you time versus manual logging.

Match the gun to the job

  • Bullpens: Portable pocket units with app logging (Pocket Radar Smart Coach).
  • Games/showcases: Pro beams with side-angle tolerance (Stalker Sport 2, Pro II).
  • Youth/rec: Budget guns are fine if you stand behind the catcher and keep batteries fresh.

See our full radar gun rankings for accuracy, range, and price comparisons.

Durability & support

Look for replaceable batteries or robust rechargeable packs, rubberized housings, and warranty support with quick turnaround—key for tournament-heavy seasons.
