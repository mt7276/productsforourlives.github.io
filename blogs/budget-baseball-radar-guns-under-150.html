Best Budget Baseball Radar Guns Under $150

Accuracy expectations and setup tips for affordable speed guns

Top budget picks

  • Bushnell Velocity: Best under $150; needs straight-behind alignment for tight reads.
  • Pocket Radar Ball Coach (used/refurb): Often close to $150 on sale; excellent accuracy in a tiny form factor.
  • General lesson: Budget guns work when you control angle and battery quality.

Get the most accurate readings

Stand directly behind the catcher, keep within 10° of flight, and avoid chain-link interference. Replace alkaline batteries early—voltage drop shows up as low readings.

When to upgrade

If you need consistent side-angle reads, instant acquisition in games, or app logging for showcases, move to pro-grade units in our radar gun guide.
